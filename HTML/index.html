Welcome to VitaSwiftAI

VitaSwiftAI is dedicated to helping computer science students streamline their job application process. We leverage cutting-edge technology and AI to automate the submission of hundreds to thousands of job applications, saving you valuable time and effort.

Our mission is to empower students to focus on their studies and personal growth while we handle the tedious task of job applications. With VitaSwiftAI, you can apply to more jobs in less time, increasing your chances of landing your dream job.

Features

  • Automated Job Applications: Submit multiple applications with a single click.
  • AI-Assisted Data Mapping: Ensure your applications are tailored to each job.
  • Secure Data Storage: Keep your personal information safe and secure.
  • Customizable Application Templates: Use templates to streamline your application process.
  • Real-Time Application Tracking: Monitor the status of your applications in real-time.
